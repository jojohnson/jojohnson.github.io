How COLORS Make You Feel

The color RED evokes feelings of warmth, love and passion.

ORANGE

The color ORANGE exhibits feelings of energy and excitement.

YELLOW

The color YELLOW generates feelings of happiness and boldness.

GREEN

The color GREEN gives feelings of good health and nature.

BLUE

The color BLUE evokes feelings of clean, soothing freshness.

PURPLE

The color PURPLE exhibits feelings of high quality and luxury.

WHITE

The color WHITE generates feelings of openness and purity.

GRAY

The color GRAY gives feelings of calm and neutrality.

BLACK

The color BLACK evokes feelings of strength and cohesion.

BROWN

The color BROWN exhibits feelings of comfort and home.

PINK

The color PINK generates feelings of femininity and softness.
